Let's Know about Italy Colosseum

  • Wonder's Name: Italy Colosseum
  • Location: Regio III Isis et Serapis, Rome, Italy
  • Place and Others: The Colosseum is an elliptical amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome, Italy, just east of the Roman Forum.
    It is the largest ancient amphitheatre ever built, and is still the largest standing amphitheatre in the world, despite its age.z
